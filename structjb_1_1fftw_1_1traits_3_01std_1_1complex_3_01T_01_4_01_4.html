Detailed Description

template<typename T>
struct jb::fftw::traits< std::complex< T > >

Define traits for std::complex<T> in terms of the traits for T.

The traits for a std::complex type are the same as the traits for the underlying precision of the complex type instantiation. The objective is to simplify/normalize the interface into FFTW3, so all uses of FFTW look similar in C++.
