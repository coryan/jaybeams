20 /**
21  * Maintain the ITCH-5.0 order book for a single security.
22  *
23  * ITCH-5.0, like other market data feeds provide order-by-order
24  * detail, that is, the feed includes a message for each order
25  * received by the exchange, as well as the changes to these
26  * orders, i.e. when the execute, when their quantity (and/or price)
27  * is modified, and when they are canceled.. Such feeds are sometimes
28  * called Level III feeds. Typically only orders that do not
29  * immediately execute in full are included in the feed.
30  *
31  * There is substantial variation in the format of the messages, and
32  * some variation as to whether executions are represented differently
33  * than a partial cancel, and whether changes in price are allowed or
34  * create new order ids.
35  *
36  * This class receives a stream of (unnormalized) ITCH-5.0 messages
37  * for a single security, and organizes the orders in a
38  * book, i.e. a data structure where orders at the same price are
39  * grouped together, and one can quickly ask:
40  *
41  * - What is the best bid (highest price of BUY orders) and what is
42  * the total quantity available at that price?
43  * - What is the best offer (lowest price of SELL orders) and what is
44  * the total quantity available at that price?
45  */
