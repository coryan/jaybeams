31 /**
32  * Maintain the ITCH-5.0 order book for a single security.
33  *
34  * ITCH-5.0, like other market data feeds provide order-by-order
35  * detail, that is, the feed includes a message for each order
36  * received by the exchange, as well as the changes to these
37  * orders, i.e. when the execute, when their quantity (and/or price)
38  * is modified, and when they are canceled.. Such feeds are sometimes
39  * called Level III feeds. Typically only orders that do not
40  * immediately execute in full are included in the feed.
41  *
42  * There is substantial variation in the format of the messages, and
43  * some variation as to whether executions are represented differently
44  * than a partial cancel, and whether changes in price are allowed or
45  * create new order ids.
46  *
47  * This class receives a stream of (unnormalized) ITCH-5.0 messages
48  * for a single security, and organizes the orders in a
49  * book, i.e. a data structure where orders at the same price are
50  * grouped together, and one can quickly ask:
51  *
52  * - What is the best bid (highest price of BUY orders) and what is
53  * the total quantity available at that price?
54  * - What is the best offer (lowest price of SELL orders) and what is
55  * the total quantity available at that price?
56  *
57  * This is a template class.
58  * @tparam book_type defines data structure type of price book,
59  * both sides buy and sell. Must be compatible with jb::itch5::map_price
60  */
61
